RSI

RSI is a technical indicator used to measure the magnitude of recent price changes to evaluate overbought or oversold conditions in a cryptocurrency. This section explains how traders use RSI to make informed decisions about buying or selling assets.

Click
SMA 111

SMA111 is a moving average calculated by adding the closing prices of a cryptocurrency over the last 111 periods and dividing the sum by 111. It helps traders identify trends and potential reversal points in the market.

Click
SMA Cross

SMACross refers to the strategy of using two different moving averages, such as the SMA50 and SMA200, to identify potential buy or sell signals in the market. This section explains how traders use SMACross to capture trends and trading opportunities.

Click
Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands are a technical analysis tool that consists of a moving average and two standard deviation bands above and below the moving average. This section explains how traders use Bollinger Bands to identify volatility and potential price reversal points in the market.

Click
Binance Order Book

The order book displays a real-time list of buy and sell orders for a particular cryptocurrency, organized by price level. Traders use the order book to gauge market depth and liquidity, helping them make informed trading decisions.

APR calculator

The APR Calculator helps users calculate the annualized percentage rate of return on their cryptocurrency investments, taking into account factors such as compounding interest and holding period. This section empowers users to make informed decisions about their investment strategies.
