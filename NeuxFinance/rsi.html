Usage

Now we come to the most critical point of the job, namely "How to interpret RSI?" Let's start with the simplest interpretation first. As it is known, RSI oscillates between 0 and 100, and an RSI that cuts the value of 30 from bottom to top is interpreted as prices may start to rise; An RSI that cuts 70 from top to bottom is interpreted as prices may start to fall.
